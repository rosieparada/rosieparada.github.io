About Me

“I was born and raised in El Salvador and am very proud to live in Wellington such a beautiful, charismatic city. The majority of my life I was a test analyst for a bank, competing up to World Class Level.
I'm a foodie, meaning Wellington is the perfect place for me! In the weekends I enjoy trying new cafes and restaurants out, catching up with friends and spending time with my close family.”
Travel is a huge passion for me too. I've visited 6 countries so far and have many more on my bucket list! I really love to immerse myself in new cultures, try out their unique cuisines and embrace the beauty of different places.“
